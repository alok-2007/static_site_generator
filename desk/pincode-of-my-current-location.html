Frequently Asked Questions

How does this Current Location pincode feature work?

When you click the "Show My Current Location Pincode" button, your browser asks for permission to access your location. Once granted, we use geolocation APIs to determine your address and extract the relevant pincode.

Is this feature accurate?

Accuracy depends on your device's GPS and internet connection. Usually, modern devices give accurate results within a few meters.

Do I need to install anything?

No, this service works directly in your browser. Make sure your location service is enabled and you allow permission when prompted.

Is my location data stored?

No, We only use your location temporarily to fetch your pincode. It is not stored or tracked by SearchPINcode.in.
