Privacy Policy

At SearchPincode.in, your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

1. Location Data: With your permission, we use your device’s geolocation to determine your current location and provide relevant pincode details using Google Maps APIs. This data is used only for this purpose and is never stored or shared.

2. Cookies & Analytics: We may use cookies and tools like Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This includes anonymous data such as browser type, time spent, and pages visited — all used to improve user experience.

3. Third-Party Tools: Some features, like Google Custom Search, may involve third-party services. These tools may collect their own data — please refer to their respective privacy policies.

4. Data Sharing: We do not sell or share any personal data with third parties. Your privacy is a priority.

5. Changes to This Policy: This policy may be updated from time to time. We encourage you to check this page for any changes.

If you have any questions or feedback, feel free to contact us.
